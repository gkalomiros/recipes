# Arroz Rojo

## Ingredients

* 2-3 tbs olive oil
* 1/2 white onion, chopped
* 2 garlic cloves, crushed and minced
* 2 cups long grain white rice
* 1/4 cup tomato paste
* 8 oz crushed tomatoes
* 3 cups chicken stock, hot
* 2-3 cilantro sprigs

## Directions

1. Heat olive oil in heavy bottomed pot or dutch oven over medium heat
2. In a separate pot, combine crushed tomato and stock over high heat to bring to simmering
3. Add onion and garlic, saute until softened, but not browned, 5 minutes
4. Add rice, stir to coat and toast about another 5 minutes until color just starts to turn
5. Add tomato paste, stir to coat, and brown for about 2 minutes
6. Pour stock mixture over rice, stir a little to loosen rice from bottom, and add cilantro over top
7. Bring up to a boil, cover, reduce heat to low, and allow to steam for 15-18 minutes, there should be no visible liquid and little steam
8. Remove from heat, allow to rest another 10 minutes
9. Remove lid, remove cilantro, fluff with a fork
