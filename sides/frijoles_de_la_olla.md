# Frijoles de la Olla

## Ingredients

* 1 lb dried pinto beans
* 1/2 white onion, halved
* 2-3 garlic cloves, smashed
* 2 tbs lard, bacon drippings, or olive oil
* 2-3 leaves epazote or bay
* 1-2 tsp salt
* 1 serrano

## Directions

1. Sort the beans, discarding any that are cracked, broken, split, or shriveled
2. Place the beans in a medium bowl and cover with a couple inches of water
3. Discard any beans that float, be sure the move the beans around to shake free any that float
4. Drain, discarding the water
5. Put the beans, onion, garlic, and fat into a heavy bottomed stock pot or large dutch oven
6. Cover with 1-2 inches of cold water
7. Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer, cover, and allow to simmer for 1-1/2 hours
8. Check periodically to see if more water needs to be added
9. After 1-1/2 hours, put a slit in the side of the serrano, and add it, the leaves, and salt to the pot
10. Return cover and simmer another 30 minutes
