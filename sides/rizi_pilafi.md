# Rizi Pilafi (Greek Rice Pilaf)

## Ingredients

* olive oil
* 1 yellow onion, chopped
* 1-2 garlic cloves, smashed and minced
* 2 cups long grain rice
* 1/2 cup kritharaki (or orzo)
* 2 lemons, zested and juiced
* 2 cups chicken stock

## Directions

1. Rinse rice until mostly clear
2. Soak rice in cold water for 15-20 minutes, drain
3. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat
4. Saute onion to soften, 3-5 minutes
5. Add garlic and pasta, saute another 1-2 minutes
6. Add rice, toss to coat
7. Add lemon juice and broth
8. Bring to a boil, reduce to low, cover with lid, simmer 20 minutes
9. Remove from heat with lid on, and allow to continue steaming for 10 minutes
10. Remove lid, add lemon zest, fluff, and serve
