# Black-Eyed Peas

## Ingredients

* 1 lb dried black-eyed peas
* 1 quart chicken stock
* 1 lb bacon, chopped
* 1 onion, chopped
* 2 tbs red wine vinegar
* 4 garlic cloves, crushed
* 1 tbs red pepper flakes

## Directions

* put peas into a stock pot, covering with 2 inches of water
* bring to a boil, and boil for 2 minutes
* turn heat off, leave covered for 1 hour
* remove peas from pot, rinse and set aside
* dry the pot and heat over medium-high with a little oil
* add bacon and onions, saute for 10 minutes to soften onion and brown bacon
* add garlic and continue to saute for 1-2 minutes until fragrant
* add peas, stock, vinegar, and pepper flakes
* bring to boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 1 hour
