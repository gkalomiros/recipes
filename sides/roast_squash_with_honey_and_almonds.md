# Roasted Squash with Honey and Almonds

## Ingredients

* 1 butternut squash
* 1/2 cup honey
* 4 tbs butter, melted
* 1/3 cup sliced almonds
* 1/4 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400&deg;
2. Cut the squash across the middle then down both lengths to make eight pieces
3. Scoop out the seeds and discard
4. Combine the honey, butter, almonds, and cinnamon in a small bowl
5. Arrange the squash on a baking sheet or dish, pouring the mixture over it
6. Cook the squash for 45 minutes until soft and golden
