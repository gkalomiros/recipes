# Virile Chickpeas

## Ingredients

* 2 tbs olive oil
* 2 garlic cloves, crushed and minced
* 1 tbs fresh rosemary, minced
* 1 lemon, zested and juiced
* 2 15oz cans of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
* 2 cups baby spinach, chopped
* 1 cup chicken stock
* 1 tsp pepper
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat
2. Saute garlic and rosemary until fragrant, about 2 minutes
3. Add lemon zest and chickpeas, cook another 3-5 minutes to heat through
4. Stir in lemon juice, stock, salt, pepper, and spinach
5. Cook, stirring until little or no more steam is being released
6. Remove from heat, place in bowl, garnished with parsley
