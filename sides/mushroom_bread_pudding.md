# Mushroom Bread Pudding

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup butter
* 2 onions, chopped
* 8 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
* 2 tbs rosemary
* 1 tbs sage
* 1/2 tbs thyme
* 1 tsp salt

* 4 shallots, chopped
* 1 lb mixed mushrooms, coarsely chopped
* 2 eggs, lightly beaten
* 1 lb stale bread, cubed
* 3 cups chicken stock

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg; and butter a 9x13 baking dish
2. Melt 1 tbs of the butter in skillet over medium-high heat
3. Add onions and shallots, stirring until softened, about 5 minutes
4. Add garlic, rosemary, sage, thyme, and salt
5. Continue cooking another 2 minutes until fragrant
6. Add remaining butter, stirring until fully melted
7. Turn off heat, add stock, stir well
8. Put bread cubes into a bowl, cover with mixture, add mushrooms, then eggs
9. Mix well by hand until bread has fully absorbed moisture
10. Pour mixture into baking dish. Pack down, removing air, for a denser pudding.
11. Bake for 45 minutes
