# Charros

## Ingredients

* 1 pot [frijoles de la olla](/sides/frijoles_de_la_olla.md)
* 1 lb bacon, chopped
* 1 lb loose Mexican chorizo, broken up
* 1 tbs oregano (Mexican if you can get it)
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp paprika
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
* 1 jalepe&ntilde;o, chopped
* 1-2 chipotle, chopped
* 1 16 oz can fire-roasted crushed tomato

## Directions

1. Bring beans to a low simmer in a large pot
2. Add bacon to a large sauce pan over medium-low heat
3. Sweat fat out of bacon until little steam is being released
4. Remove the bacon from the pan with a slotted spoon and add to pot
5. Turn heat up on bacon fat to medium and use it to cook the chorizo through, about 5 minutes
6. Add in the seasonings, stirring to coat, and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes
7. Add in the tomato, cilantro, jalepe&ntilde;o, and chipotle
8. Bring up to a boil, then reduce to low simmer, and cook for about 15 minutes
9. Transfer meat sauce to beans, stir through, and allow to simmer another 5 minutes
