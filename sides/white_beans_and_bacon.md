# White Beans and Bacon

## Ingredients

* 4 rashers of bacon, chopped
* 1 small onion, chopped
* 1 head endive, chopped
* 2 cloves garlic, chopped
* 1 16oz can white beans, drained and rinsed
* salt and black pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook bacon in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until crispy, set aside
2. Add onion and saute until tender, about 10 minutes
3. Add half of endive leaves and cover until wilted, about 5 minutes
4. Add garlic and remaining endive, cover, and cook another 5 minutes
5. Return bacon and add beans, stirring until beans are heated through
6. Season with salt and pepper and serve
