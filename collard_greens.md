# Collard Greens

## Ingredients

* 2 lbs collard greens, avoid wilted or yellowing leaves
* 1 quart chicken stock
* 1 hamhock
* 1 onion, chopped
* 4 garlic, crushed
* 1 tbs red pepper flakes
* 3 tbs red wine vinegar
* 1 tbs sugar
* 1/2 tsp baking soda

## Directions

1. Remove collard leaves from stems, rinse, shake off, and leave to dry
2. In a large pot, add a little oil and saute onions over medium-high until softened, 5-10 min
3. Add garlic until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes
4. Add stock and hamhock, bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer
5. Slowing incorporate collard leaves in batches until they wilt
6. Add remaining ingredients, stir, then leave to simmer at least 2 hours
