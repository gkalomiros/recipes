# Southern Red Rice

## Ingredients

* 1 lb bacon, chopped
* 1 onion, chopped
* 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed and minced
* 2 cans of tomato paste
* 1 quart of chicken stock
* 1 tbs salt
* 2 cups of long grain rice

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg;
2. Put bacon into a cold dutch oven
3. Set dutch oven to medium heat and brown bacon to render
4. Remove bacon with slotted spoon and set aside
5. Add onion to dutch oven to saute for 5-10 minutes
6. Add garlic and saute for another 1-2 minutes
7. Add tomato paste to brown for 2-3 minutes
8. Add stock and stir until paste is dissolved
9. Add salt and rice
10. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, simmer uncovered for 10 minutes
11. Cover the dutch oven and bake for 45 minutes
