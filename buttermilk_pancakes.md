# Buttermilk Pancakes

## Ingredients

### Dry

* 4 cups AP flour
* 4 tsp baking powder
* 2 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp salt

### Wet

* 1/2 cup clarified butter
* 1 quart buttermilk
* 2/3 cup whole milk
* 4 eggs
* 1/4 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat (should bead/sizzle drips of water)
2. Whisk together dry ingredients into a large bowl
3. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat
4. Turn off heat and whisk in remaining wet ingredients
5. Fold wet ingredients into dry, avoid over stirring, some small lumps are okay
6. Drop 1/2 cup scoops of batter onto skillet
7. When sides pull away and bubble form on edges, flip
8. Cook for a couple more minutes on flipped side, set aside and cook next scoops
