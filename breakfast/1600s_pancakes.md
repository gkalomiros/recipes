# Pancakes (from the 17th Century England)

## Ingredients

* 6 large eggs
* 1 pint heavy cream
* 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
* 2 tsp nutmeg
* 2 cups AP flour

## Directions

1. Whisk eggs in large mixing bowl until frothy
2. Whisk in cream until smooth
3. Whisk in sugar and nutmeg
4. Fold in flour until just combined
5. Cover bowl and rest batter in refrigerator for 30 minutes
6. Heat griddle over medium low heat
7. Melt cooking fat, typically butter but ghee works too, on griddle
8. Ladle batches of pancakes onto griddle
9. Cook for 2-4 minutes on first side before flipping and cooking another 2
10. Add more butter to griddle as needed between batches
