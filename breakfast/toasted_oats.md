# Toasted Oats

## Ingredients

### Porridge

* 1 tbs butter
* 1 cup steel-cut oats
* 3 cups boiling water
* 3/4 cup whole milk

## Directions

1. Melt butter in large saucepan over medium-high heat
2. Add oats, stir to coat, and continue to stir while toasting for 2-4 minutes
3. Pour in boiling water, stir well, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes
4. Slowly stir milk into porridge, continue to simmer, uncovered, for another 10 minutes
