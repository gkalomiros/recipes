# Berry &amp; Coconut Oatmeal

## Ingredients

* 1 tbs coconut oil (or butter)
* 1 cup steel cut oats
* 2 tbs unsweetened coconut flakes
* 2 cups water
* 1 cup whole milk
* 1/4 cup plain yogurt
* 1 cup fresh berries, like blueberries
* honey

## Directions

1. Heat the cooking fat in medium saucepan over medium-low heat
2. Add oats and coconut, stir to coat, and toast for 3-4 minutes while stirring occasionally
3. While toasting oats, combine water and milk separately, and bring to a simmer
4. Once the oats are toasted and the liquids are bubbling, pour the liquids over the oats
5. Reduce to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, occasionally stirring to prevent sticking
6. Remove lid and continue simmering for up to 10 minutes until desired consistency is reached
7. Portion into bowls, swirl in a portion of yogurt, top with berries, and drizzle with honey
