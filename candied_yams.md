# Candied Yams

## Ingredients

* yams
* brown sugar
* cinnamon
* maple syrup
* orange
* pecans
* marshmallows
* Grand Marnier
* butter

## Directions

1. roast yams at 350&deg; for ~45 minutes to soften
2. cool for 5-10 minutes, then peel
3. cut into thick slices
4. lay in single layer into buttered glass baking dish
5. sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon, syrup, and orange juice
6. add butter knobs, pecans, splash of liquor, and orange zest
7. return to oven until bubbling
8. add marshmallows
9. return to oven until marshmallows are light brown
