# Macadamia Butter

## Ingredients

* 10 oz/ 2 cups macadamia nuts
* 1/3 cup of neutral or nut oil
* 2 tbs honey

## Directions

1. warm the honey to loosen the viscosity, about 15 seconds in a microwave
2. put nuts into a food processor and pulse about 5 seconds to chop
3. add honey and oil and run processor until desired smoothness
