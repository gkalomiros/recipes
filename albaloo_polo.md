# Albaloo Polo (Persian Sour Cherry Rice)

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups basmati rice
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1/4 cup melted ghee
* 12 oz sour cherries in light syrup, drained but syrup reserved
* 1/2 tsp saffron soaked in 2 tbs hot water

## Directions

1. soak rice, covered with 1 inch of water for 2 hours, drain
2. add water to reserved syrup until it combines to 1/2 cup
3. bring syrup and sugar to boil over medium-high heat
4. reduce to low and simmer for 30 minutes, set aside
5. boil 3 cups of water in a dutch oven
6. add rice and par-boil over medium heat for 8 minutes
7. drain, rinse with cold water, drain again, halve, and set aside
8. put half the butter into the dutch oven and melt over high heat
9. add half the rice and stir in the saffron
10. add half the cherries
11. Add remaining rice and butter, stirring just the top half
12. add remaining cherries on top
13. cover with tea towl and lid and cook on high for 10 minutes
14. turn heat to lowest setting and continue to cook for 20 minutes
15. remove from heat and allow to rest for 5 minutes
16. drizzle syrup over rice and serve
