# Lentil Soup

## Ingredients

* 1 lb of brown or green dry lentils
* 2 tbs olive oil
* 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
* 3 bay leaves
* 1 can crushed tomatoes
* 1 quart vegetable stock
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp black pepper

## Finishing

* 2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
* 2 tbs red wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Put lentils and enough water to cover by an inch
2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer for 15 minutes
3. Drain the lentils, dicarding the water, then rinse
4. Dry the pot, add oil, and saute garlic until fragrant, ~2 minutes
5. Add stock, tomatoes, lentils, bay leaves, salt, pepper and enough additional water to cover by an inch
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer for 45 minutes
7. Remove from heat, top with olive oil and vinegar before serving
