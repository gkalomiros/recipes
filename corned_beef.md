# Corned Beef

## Ingredients

* 1 qt water
* 3 lbs ice
* 1 4-5 lb beef brisket, trimmed

## Pickling Mix

* 12 oz kosher salt
* 1/2 cup light brown sugar
* 4 tsp pink salt
* 1 cinnamon stick, broken up
* 1 tsp mustard seeds
* 1 tsp black peppercorns
* 8 whole cloves
* 8 whole allspice berries
* 12 whole juniper berries
* 2 bay leaves, crumbled
* 1/2 tsp ground ginger

## Slow-Cooker

* 1 onion, quartered
* 1 carrot, coarsely chopped
* 1 stalk celery, coarsely chopped

## Directions

### Brining

1. Place the 1 quart of water in stock pot with pickling mix, cook over high heat until salt and sugar have fully dissolved
2. Remove the pot from heat and add ice, stirring until melted. Get heat down to at least 45&deg;, refrigerate if needed
3. Place brisket in 2 gallon plastic ziplock and add brine. Seal, and seal again in a second bag.
4. Lay flat in refrigerator for 5 days, turning daily. The meat should be fully submerged.

### Cooking

1. After brining, remove brisket from bags and rinse in cool water (must flush any pink salt)
2. Place brisket in slow cooker with onion, carrot, and celery
3. Cover with water by about an inch
4. Cover cooker and set for 8 hours
5. Remove and carve against grain
