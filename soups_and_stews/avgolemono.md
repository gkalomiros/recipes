# Avgolemono

## Ingredients

* 2 quarts chicken stock
* 1 whole roasted chicken
* 1/2 cup rice
* 3 eggs, separated
* 3 lemons, juiced
* 1 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. Add stock, rice, and pepper to pot
2. Bring to boil, reduce to low simmer 20 minutes
3. Process the chicen: Cut away the breats and thighs. Remove the skin and bones. Shred the meat.
4. Add the shredded meat to the pot
5. Take pot off of heat
6. In a medium bowl, beat the egg whites until frothy
7. While continuing to beat the eggs, add one yolk at a time, then the lemon juice
8. While continuing to beat the eggs, temper them by slowly adding a cup of broth a quarter at a time
9. Incorporate the eggs into the soup and serve
