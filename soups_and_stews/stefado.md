# Stefado

## Ingredients

* 3 lb beef chuck
* 1/4 cup tomato paste
* 4 garlic cloves, crushed and minced
* 1 cup red wine
* 2 cups beef stock
* 2 cups tomato sauce
* 3 lb white perl onions, peeled
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 tsp nutmeg
* 1/2 tsp oregano
* salt, pepper, bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cut the chuck into large chunks, removing connective tissue and trimming large bits of fat
2. In a large pot, brown the meat, then set aside
3. Reduce heat to medium and add garlic, sautee 1-2 minutes
4. Add tomato paste to poy, stirring to brown another 1-2 minutes
5. Add red wine to pot to deglaze, scraping bottom until incorporated
6. Return meat and add remaining ingredients to pot
7. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, covered, for 1 hour
8. Remove bay leaf, serve with crusty white bread
