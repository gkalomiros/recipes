# Croutons

## Ingredients

* 1 stale sweet baguette, cubed
* 1/4 cup olive oil
* 2 tbs butter, melted
* 2+ garlic cloves, crushed and minced
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp italian seasoning

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg;
2. Combine fats and seasonings well in a large bowl
3. Add bread cubes and toss to coat
4. Add cubes in single layer to parchment paper-lined baking sheet
5. Bake for 15 minutes
