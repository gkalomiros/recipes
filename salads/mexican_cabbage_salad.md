# Mexican Cabbage Salad

## Ingredients

* 1 head cabbage, shredded
* 3-4 carrots, sliced thin
* 4-5 radishes, sliced thin
* 2 pints cherry tomatoes, or 2-3 larger tomatoes diced
* 1/2 onion, white or red, sliced thin
* 1/2 bunch green onion, sexy-sliced

## Directions

* Divide the non-cabbage vegetables
* Toss the cabbage and half the other vegetables in a bowl with some salad dressing
* Toss the remaining vegetables with a little dressing, then distribute over cabbage mix

## Notes

* This salad is best when colorful. If possible, opt for both green and red cabbage, rainbow carrots, Easter egg radishes, and heirloom tomatoes
