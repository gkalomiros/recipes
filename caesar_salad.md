# Caesar Salad

## Ingredients

* 2 or 3 heads Romain lettuce, washed, well-dried, and torn into chunks
* 1/4 cup fresh grated Parmesean cheese
* 1 tbs black pepper
* [caesar salad dressing](caesar_salad_dressing.md)
* [croutons](croutons.md)

## Directions

1. In a small plastic baggie, shake together cheese and pepper
2. In a large bowl, toss together lettuce, half the croutons, half the cheese, and half the dressing
3. Top the salad with the remaining croutons and cheese
4. Serve with remaining dressing on the side
