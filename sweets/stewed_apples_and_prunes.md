# Stewed Apples &amp; Prunes

## Ingredients

* 1 cup hard apple cider
* 4 whole cloves
* 1" cinnamon stick
* 3 green apples, cored and quartered, peel on
* a dozen pitted prunes

## Directions

1. Add cider, cloves, and cinnamon to a medium pot set to boil
2. Once boiling, set apples, cut-side down, into bottom of pot
3. Top apples with prunes
4. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes, or until liquid is mostly reduced
