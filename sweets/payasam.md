# Payasam

## Ingredients

* 1 heaping 1/2 cup dry white rice
* 1 qt whole milk
* 1 cup grated jaggery (or 1/3 cup sugar and 2 tbs molasses)
* 3/4 tsp ground cardamom
* 10 to 12 threads saffron
* 1/4 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice well, then put in a bowl soaking in clean, cold water for 30 minutes
2. Rinse rice again and drain
3. Pour milk into a sauce pan and bring to a simmer over medium high heat
4. Reduce heat to medium low and add rice
5. Stirring occasionally, simmer rice for 20 minutes until rice can easily be mushed with fingers
6. Mix in sugar, cardamom, saffron, and salt until sugar is dissolved
7. Simmer another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
8. Remove from heat and allow to cool for several minutes to thicken
9. Servce warm or chilled
