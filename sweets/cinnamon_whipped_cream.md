# Cinnamon Whipped Cream

## Ingredients

* 1 cup heavy cream
* 3 tbs powdered sugar
* 1 tsp vanilla
* 1 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put large mixing bowl and whisk, beaters, or whisk attachment in freezer for about 30 minutes
2. Pour heavy cream into the bowl and begin beating at high until frothy, about 1 minute
3. Reduce to medium and slowly add sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla
4. Continue beating on medium setting until soft peaks form, about 2 minutes
