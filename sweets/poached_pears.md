# Poached Pears

## Ingredients

* 4 large, firm pears, peeled, halved, and cored
* 2 cups hard cider or white wine
* 1/4 cup sugar
* 1 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Set alcohol, sugar, and cinnamon to boil in a wide saucepan over medium-high heat
2. Place pears, cut-side down, in pan, reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes
3. Flip pears, return lid, and simmer another 10 minutes
4. Remove with slotted spoon
