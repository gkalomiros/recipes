# Apple Hand Pies

## Ingredients

### Filling

* 8 tart apples, peeled, cored, and chopped, smaller is better
1 cup sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
pinch ground cloves
3 tbs butter, melted
2 tbs honey

### Crust

* 1/3 [short crust pastry](/breads/short_crust_pastry.md)
1 tbs sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

### Filling

1. Combine sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves into a large mixing bowl
2. Add the apple and toss well to coat
3. Top with butter and honey,toss more to coat, and set aside
4. Sprinkle flour on work surface and roll dough out to about 1/4" thickness

### Assembly

1. Preheat oven to 375&deg;
2. Lightly flour working surface
3. Roll out the dough to 1/4" - 1/8". Thinner if they're going to be eaten right away; thicker for travel
4. Use a cutter to make individual rounds, 2 for each pie
5. Densely pack 2 tbs of filling onto a bottom round
6. Tightly stretch a second round over top, and crimp with a fork
7. Dock a couple holes on top
8. Arrange onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet with a couple inches between each
9. Lightly brush on an egg wash and sprinkle with a cinnamon sugar mixture
10. Bake 20-25 minutes
