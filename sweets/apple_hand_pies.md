# Apple Hand Pies

Makes 3 or 4 pies

## Ingredients

### Filling

* 8 tart apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
1 cup sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
pinch ground cloves
3 tbs butter, melted
2 tbs honey

### Crust

* 1/3 [short crust pastry](/breads/short_crust_pastry.md)
1 tbs sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375&deg;
