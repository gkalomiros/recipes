# Apple Cake

## Ingredients

### Dry

* 2 cups AP flour
* 2 tsp baking soda
* 2 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp salt

### Wet

* 4 cups of peeled and sliced apples
* 1 cup chopped walnuts
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups sugar
* 1/2 cup neutral vegetable oil
* 2 tsp vanilla

### Nuts

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg; and butter a 9x15 baking pan or 10" bundt pan
2. Whisk together dry ingredients in a large bowl
3. Mix together wet ingredents in a medium bowl
4. Add wet into dry, mixing thoroughly
5. Bake for 45 - 60 minutes until passing the toothpick test
