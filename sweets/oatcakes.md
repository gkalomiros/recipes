# Oatcakes

## Ingredients

* 3 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats (not quick-cooking)
* 1 tsp salt
* 2 tbs flour
* 3 tbs honey
* 4 tbs unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
* 1/2 cup water
* 1/2 cup fresh berries
* 1/2 cup pine nuts, chopped
* jam

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg;, and grease a baking sheet
2. In a large bowl, combine the oats, salt, flour, and honey
3. Rub in the butter until you get a pebbly dough
4. Add just enough water until the dough comes together and a ball can be formed
5. Split the dough, putting half into a second bowl
6. Put the berries in one bowl and the pine nuts into the other, then mix them thoroughly
7. Either with a cutter or by hand, form 3" rounds that are about 1/4" thick
8. Place rounds on the baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes, or until lightly browned. The berry ones may need a little extra.
9. Cool on a wire rack
