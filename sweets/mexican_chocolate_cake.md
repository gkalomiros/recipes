# Mexican Chocolate Cake

## Ingredients

### Dry

* 1 3/4 cups AP flour
* 2 cups sugar
* 3/4 cups cocoa powder
* 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

### Wet

* 2 eggs
* 1 cup buttermilk
* 1/2 cup neutral oil (like canola or vegetable)
* 2 tsp vanilla
* 1 cup fresh hot coffee

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg;
2. Prepare either 2 8" cake pans or 1 9x13 dish
3. Whisk together dry ingredients into a medium mixing bowl
4. Mix wet ingredients, except for the coffee, into a small mixing bowl
5. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined
6. Mix the hot coffee into the batter, which will be runny
7. Pour the batter into the cake pans
8. Bake for 30-35 minutes for rounds or 25-30 for a sheet cake
9. Cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to finish cooling before decorating
