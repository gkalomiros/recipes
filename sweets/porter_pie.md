# Porter Pie

## Ingredients

### Fruits

* 1 1/2 cups mix of candied citrus peel, dried cherries, dried currants, golden raisins, and/or raisins
* 1 1/4 cup porter beer

### Wet

* 3/4 cup butter, room temperature
* 1 cup sugar
* 3 eggs
* 1 orange, juiced and zested

### Dry

* 1 tsp baking powder
* 2 1/2 cups AP flour
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp allspice
* 1/2 tsp nutmeg
* pinch of cloves

### Finishing

* 1/4 cup almond flakes
* 1/2 cup packed brown sugar

## Directions

1. A day ahead of baking, combine the dried fruits and 1/2 cup of beer into a small bowl and soak overnight
2. Preheat oven to 325&deg; and butter 2 1-lb. loaf pans
3. In a standing mixer on medium high, cream the butter and sugar together until pale
4. Reduce speed to medium-low, add one egg at a time until incorporated, then add in the orange juice and zest until combined
5. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients
6. Drain fruits then toss into dry ingredients to coat
7. With the processor on low, incorporate the dry ingredients to the wet in three batches
8. Add the remaining beer until incorporated and the foam dies down
9. Pour the batter into the buttered pans, shake gently to remove air, but not too much to avoid sinking the fruit
10. Sprinkle with brown sugar, then the almonds. Press down lightly to set the almonds
11. Bake 45-50 minutes, until dark brown and passes toothpick test
12. Remove, turn out, and cool
