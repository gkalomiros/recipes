# Masala Chai (serves 2)

## Ingredients

### Spices

* 3-4 cloves
* 1/2 - 3/4 cinnamon stick
* 4 green cardomom pods
* 2-4 peppercorns

### Tea

* 1 1/2 cups water
* 1 tsp ginger
* 3 tbs black tea
* 2 tbs jaggery (or dark brown sugar)

### Finishing

* 1 cup full fat milk

## Directions

1. Crush spices well in pestle or spice grinder
2. Bring water to a simmer in a medium saucepan
3. Add spices, ginger, tea, and sugar to water
4. Simmer on low/medium-low for 3 minutes
5. Stir milk into saucepan
6. Return to simmer, and whisk for 3 minutes
7. Filter and serve
