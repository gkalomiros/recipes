# Xocolatl (Aztec Chocolate)

## Ingredients

* 3/4 cup cocoa nibs
* 2 chipotles
* 1 qt water
* 1 vanilla pod, or 2 tsp vanilla
* 1/3 cup honey, or agave syrup
* 1/2 tsp allspice, ground

## Directions

1. Crush cacao until they begin to liquify, set aside
2. Crush chipotle
3. Add chipotle and water to sauce pan, bring to boil, reduce and simmer for 5 minutes
4. Strain and keep water, discard chile
5. Return water to pan, add scraped vanilla seeds, sweetener, and allspice; set to low
6. Mix water until fully combined, bring to boil, reduce to simmer, and add cocao
7. Whisk until combined then simmer for 5 minutes
8. Pour into mugs and froth with whisk or molinillo, serve
