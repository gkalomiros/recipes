# Spicy Ginger-Lime Sauce

This is good for fish, like tuna steaks

## Ingredients

* 1 bunch of cilantro, finely chopped
* 1 jalepe&ntilde;o, finely chopped, remove seeds for less heat
* 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
* 1 clove garlic, crushed and finely chopped
* 2 limes, juiced
* 2 tbs soy sauce

## Directions

1. Combine in a small bowl, mixing well
