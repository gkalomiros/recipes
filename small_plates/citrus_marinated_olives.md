# Citrus Marinated Olives

## Ingredients

* 2 tsp fennel seeds
* 1/2 lemon, zested and juiced
* 1/4 orange, zested and juiced
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 4 cups mixed olives

## Directions

1. Toast fennel seeds in a small skillet for about 30 seconds
2. Lightly crush fennel seeds
3. Combine ingredients in non-metallic bowl
4. Marinate overnight in refrigerator
5. Serve at room temperature
