# Saganaki

## Ingredients

* 1 lb graviera, kefalograviera, or kefalotyri cheese
* cold water
* flour for dredging
* olive oil for frying
* 1/4 cup Metaxa
* fresh lemons

## Directions

1. Cut cheese into 3/4-1" thick slices
2. Heat oil in a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat
3. Dip slices into cold water, dredge, and shake off excess flour
4. Lay one or two slices at a time into the pan, cooking 1-2 minutes or until golden brown
5. Flip, cook another minute or two, and flambe with a little of the Metaxa
6. Remove from the skillet, squeeze some lemon juice over it
7. Serve immediately
