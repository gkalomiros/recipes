# Chipotle Honey Glaze

## Ingredients

* 1 can chipotle in adobo sauce
* 1/2 cup honey
* 1 tbs dijon mustard
* 2 tbs neutral oil
* 1 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Add chipotle to food processor and blend until smooth
2. Warm honey in microwave to soften
3. Transfer to small bowl and whisk in remaining ingredients until emulsified
