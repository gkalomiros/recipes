# Fried Rice

## Ingredients

* 4 cups leftover rice
* 2 eggs
* 1 bunch green onions, white chopped and greens sliced on the bias
* 1/2 cup frozen peas
* 1/2 cup of meat, chopped
* 2 tbs chicken stock
* 2 tbs soy sauce
* 1 tbs mirin
* 1 tbs sesame oil
* 1 tsp MSG

## Directions

1. lightly beat eggs in bowl, scramble in pan, set aside
2. brown meat in pan, set aside
3. thaw peas, set aside
4. heat 2 tbs peanut oil in pan over medium heat
5. add rice and onion whites to pan and fry about 5 minutes
6. add meat and vegetables, stir in to warm, about 1 minute
7. add stock, soy, mirin, and MSG, stir to combine, about 1 minute
8. add eggs and sesame oil, stir to heat, about 1 minute
9. serve hot
