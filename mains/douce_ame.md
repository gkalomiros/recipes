# Douce Ame (Capon in Milk &amp; Honey)

## Ingredients

* 2 to 3 lbs chicken (or capon) cut into large pieces (legs and wings kept whole, thighs cut in half, breasts in three)
* 3 to 4 tbs olive oil, melted butter or ghee
* 2 cups whole milk
* 1 cup heavy cream
* 1/4 honey
* 2 tbs fresh parsely, chopped
* 2 tbs fresh sage, chopped
* 2 tbs fresh mint, chopped
* 1 tbs savory
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* pinch of saffron
* 1/3 cup pine nuts

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 300&deg;
2. Rub chicken with cooking fat, then lightly brown, in batches, in a skillet over medium heat
3. Set the chicken aside, keep the rendered fat
4. In a small sauce pan, combine milk, cream, honey, herbs, saffron, and salt
5. Set sauce pan to medium heat until simmering and simmer for 5 minutes
6. Add pine nuts to skillet and toast, stirring frequently, for 2 minutes
7. Layer chicken in oven-safe dish and pour milk mixture over it
8. Cover the dish and bake for 30 minutes until the chicken is cooked through
9. Serve in sauce
