# Greek Pasta Sauce

## Ingredients

* 1 jar tomato or pasta sauce
* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 onion, chopped and lightly salted
* 2 - 3 garlic cloves, crushed
* 1 cup red wine
* 1/2 cinnamon stick
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 1-2 bay leaves
* salt to taste

## Directions

1. Heat some olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat
2. Put the meat in the pan, breaking it into small chunks, to brown
3. Remove the meat, setting it aside
4. Put the onions into the pan and saute to soften, about 4-5 minutes
5. Add the garlic and saute another 1-2 minutes until fragrent
6. Drain off the fat from the pan, then add the meat back in
7. Reduce the heat to medium
8. Add the wine a litle at a time, scraping up the bits stuck to the bottom of the pan
9. Simmer the wine sauce until it reduces a little
10. Pour in the tomato sauce and add the cinnamon, oregano, and bay leaves
11. Turn up heat to start a low boil, then reduce to low and simmer 5 minutes
12. Taste and add salt as needed, continue simmering at least 15 minutes, more is better
13. Remove the cinnamon and bay leaves
