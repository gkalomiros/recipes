# Steak and Ale Pie

## Ingredients

* 1/4 lb bacon, chopped
* 2 lbs beef chuck, trimmed and diced
* 4 tbs butter
* 2 onions, chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
* 1 tbs fresh rosemary, minced
* 1/2 tbs thyme
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1/2 lb carrots, peeled and diced
* 2 cups brown ale
* 2 1/4 cup beef stock
* 1 batch [short crust pastry dough](/breads/short_crust_pastry_dough.md)
* 2 tbs flour
* 1/4 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Fry bacon in a skillet over medium heat until rendered
2. Remove the bacon with a slotted spoon and set aside
3. Brown the chuck in batches, also setting aside
4. Melt butter in skillet and add onions to soften, about 5 minutes
5. Add garlic, rosemary, thyme, and pepper, stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes
6. Add beef, onion mixture, carrots, ale, and stock into a dutch oven
7. Bring to a boil, reduce to a medium-low and simmer, covered, for 90 minutes
8. Preheat oven to 400&deg;
9. Divide pastry in half, roll out half until large enough o fill the bottom of a greased pastry dish
10. Dock the bottom
11. In small bowl, whisk together flour and cold water until smooth
12. Add floured water to dutch oven, mixing thoroughly to thicken
13. Add bacon to dutch oven
14. Pour filling into casserole until about 3/4 filled
15. Roll out other half of dough until large enough to cover pie
16. Pinch pie closed, trim, slice a couple vents, paint with an egg wash, and place on baking sheet
17. Bake pie for 30-35 minutes, until crust is a dark, golden brown
