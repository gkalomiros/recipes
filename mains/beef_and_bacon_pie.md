# Beef and Bacon Pie

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup bacon, chopped
* 1 1/2 lb beef chuck, chopped
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
* 1/2 cup prunes
* 1/2 cup raisins
* 1/2 cup dates, chopped
* 1 cup beef stock
* 2-3 tbs flour
* 1 batch short crust pastry dough
* 1 egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375&deg;
2. In a saucepan over medium heat, cook bacon
3. Add beef, spices, vinegar, and fruits, and stock
4. Scrape up any frond from pan
5. Reduce to low, and slowly incorporate flour to form a gravy
6. Turn off heat and allow to cool
7. Roll out two circles with the pastry dough, one with about 2/3 of the dough
8. Line a 9" pie dish with the larger round, fill, then top with the smaller round and crimp closed
9. Brush with the beaten egg, then bake 40 minutes
