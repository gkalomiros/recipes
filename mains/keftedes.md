# Keftedes

## Ingredients

* 1 lb. ground beef
* 1/2 cup ground onion
* 1 tbs minced garlic
* 1/4 tsp oregano
* 1/8 tsp mint
* salt and pepper to taste
* 1/2 cup bread crumbs
* 1/4 cup dry white wine
* 1 shot ouzo
* 1 egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Mix, by hand, meat, onion, garlic, herbs, and spices in a mixing bowl
2. Moisten the bread crumbs with the wine and ouzo
3. Mix meat, bread, and egg
4. Form patties or meat balls, then pan fry and serve
