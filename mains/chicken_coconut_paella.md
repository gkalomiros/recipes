# Chicken and Coconut "Paella"

## Ingredients

### Dry Rub

* 1 tbs salt
* 1 tsp peppercorns
* 1/4 cup coriander seed
* 8 chicken thighs

### Saute

* 1 onion, minced
* 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
* 1 bay leaf

### Paella

* 1 cup basmati rice
* 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
* 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
* 1 lemon zested

### Pea Salad

* 1 cup fresh or thawed green peas
* small handfull fresh mint leaves, chopped
* 1 bunch watercress, chopped
* juice from zested lemon
* 3 tbs olive oil

### Garnish

* mint leaves
* lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400&deg;
2. Grind dry rub ingredients with pestle and mortar
3. Apply the dry rub to the chicken
4. Heat oiled dutch oven over medium-high heat
5. Combine the broth, coconut milk, and lemon zest in a small mixing bowl
6. Brown the chicken pieces in batches, skin-down first, then set aside
7. Reduce heat to medium, add a little more oil and the onion, ginger and bay
8. Stir constantly for 3-4 minutes until onion is softened
9. Add rice and stir a couple minutes to coat
10. Stir in liquids, and scraping up the bottom of the dutch oven
11. Turn up the heat to bring to a simmer
12. Top with the chicken, skin-side up, and put into oven, uncovered
13. Bake for 30 minutes
14. While baking, combine lemon juice, oil, salt, and pepper into a mixing bowl
15. Add the peas, mint, and watercross, and toss to coat
16. Serve by plating a serving of rice with a piece of chicken garnished with mint and lemon, and sided with salad
