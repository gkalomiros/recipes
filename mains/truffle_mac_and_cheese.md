# Truffled Macaroni &amp; Cheese

## Ingredients

### Pasta

* 3/4 lbs elbow pasta
* 6 qts salted water

## Rue

* 4 tbs clarified butter
* 4 tbs flour

## Cheese Sauce

* 3 cups milk
* 3/4 cup gouda cheese, grated
* 3/4 cup gruyere cheese, grated
* 3/4 cup cheddar cheese, grated
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp granulated garlic
* 3/4 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

### Crust

* 1/4 panko crumbs
* 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese, grated

### Finishing

* 1 tbs chopped parsley
* 1 tbs truffle oil

## Directions

1. preheat oven to 350&deg;
2. put a baking dish into the oven to heat
3. in a small bowl, mix together bread crumbs and parmesan cheese, set aside
4. start cooking the pasta, if it finished before the cheese sauce is ready, drain and set aside
5. melt the butter in a large sauce pan over medium heat
6. reduce to low, and whisking constantly, slowly add flour into the butter
7. brown rue for about 5 minutes
8. heat milk, then while stirring constantly, slow add the milk until well combined
9. add seasonings and cheese, strirring until cheese is melted and incorporated
10. add drained pasta to sauce and mix
11. remove baking dish from oven and add sauced pasta, smoothing to even
12. top with panko crumbs
13. bake in oven for 15-20 minutes until lightly browned on top
14. remove from oven and rest for 10 minutes
15. top with parsley and drizzle with truffle oil
