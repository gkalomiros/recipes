# Baked Scotch Eggs

## Ingredients

* 6 eggs, soft to hard cooked and cooled
* 2 lbs breakfast sausage

### Egg Wash

* 1 egg
* 1 tbs water

### Coating

* 1 clove garlic, crushed and minced
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp paprika
* 1/4 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 tsp savory or sage, finely chopped
* 1/4 tsp rosemary, finely chopped
* 2 cups coarse breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375&deg;
2. Whisk together egg wash in a small mixing bowl
3. Whisk together the coating in a medium mixing bowl
4. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil, then top with a meat rack
5. Divide the sausage into 6 portions, flatten, and wrap each egg, covering them completely
6. For each egg, roll in egg wash, roll in coating, repeat, then place on meat rack
7. Bake for 45 minutes, turning every 15
8. Halve, sprinkle with black pepper, and side with brown, dijion, or hot mustard
