# Beef with Garlic Harvester Sauce

## Ingredients

### Roast

* 3 1/2 to 4 lbs chuck roast
* 2 tsp black pepper
* 2 tsp salt, divided
* 2 tbs olive oil
* 2 cups parsnips, chopped
* 1 large onion, finely chopped
* 1/2 cup fresh parsley, torn
* 3 cups water

### Sauce

* 6 medium eggs at room temperature
* 1/3 cup white wine vinegar
* 2 tbs garlic, crushed and minced
* 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 300&deg;
2. Rub roast with pepper and 1 tsp salt
3. Heat oil in dutch oven over medium high heat
4. Brown roast on all sides
5. Add parsnips, onion, parsley, and remaining salt to pot and cook until onion begins to brown, 10 minutes
6. Set aside vegetables and deglaze dutch oven with water
7. Set roast and juices back into dutch oven
8. Arrange vegetables around roast
9. Bring roast to simmer, cover, and transfer to oven
10. Roast for 1 hour per pound of meat
11. Remove roast from oven, remove meat and vegetables to serving dish, tent with foil, and rest it while preparing sauce
12. Scoop a cup of broth out of the dutch oven, avoiding onions if possible, and set aside
13. Before roast is done, whisk the eggs in a medium saucepan until smooth
14. Slowly whisk vinegar into eggs, then continue with garlic and pepper
15. Slowly whisk broth into eggs to temper
16. Turn pan to medium low heat and stir slowly until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes
17. Remove tin from roast, carve it into thick slices
18. Serve the slices, topped with sauce, and sided with vegetables
