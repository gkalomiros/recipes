# Beef Brisket

## Ingredients

* 1 whole beef brisket, about 10 lbs, trimmed
* 2 tbs, 2 tsp Kosher salt
* 1 tbs, 2 tsp ground black pepper
* 1 1/2 tbs, 2 tsp sweet paprika
* 1/2 cup oil
* 10 medium onions, chopped
* 2 quarts beef stock
* 4 lbs/1 dozen red potatoes, cubed to 1", skins on, and soaking in cold water

## Directions

1. Dry rub the brisket with the larger portions of salt, pepper, and paprika
2. Leaving the skins on, cut the potatoes into 1" cubes, and place in cold water
3. Preheat oven to 350&deg; with rack in the center
4. Preheat roasting pan over medium-high to high heat
5. Heat oil in pan until shimmering, the brown the meat about 3-4 minutes per side, then set aside
6. Reduce heat to medium and add onions and saute about 15 minutes until browned and very soft
7. Return meat to pan and add enough stock to just cover the meat
8. Bring to a boil, remove from heat, cover tightly, and place in oven
9. After 90 minutes of baking, drain the potatoes and place in large bowl
10. Toss the potatoes with remaining salt, pepper, and paprika
11. Remove baking lid, flip meat, add potatoes around meat, return cover
12. Bake another 45 minutes, then remove the lid, and bake another 45-75 minutes until fork-tender
13. Remove pan from oven, lift meat out onto a cutting board
14. Remove potatoes with a slotted spoon and place in serving bowl (cover with foil)
15. Carve brisket into 1/4" slices, against the meat grain
16. Place slices onto serving plate, pour some gravy over, serve with potatoes and remaining gravy on the side
