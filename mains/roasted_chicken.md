# Roasted Chicken

## Ingredients

* A whole chicken, take note of its weight
* salt, pepper
* something to stuff the cavity, like a lemon or onion

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 450&deg;
2. Thoroughly pat dry the chicken with paper towels
3. Liberally season the chicken all over with salt and pepper
4. Stuff the cavity with a lemon or onion. If a lemon is used, poke several holes to allow the juice to come out.
5. Use toothpicks to close the cavity around the stuffing
6. Place the chicken breast down into a baking dish
7. Roast the chicken for 30 minutes
8. Turn the chicken over, reduce the oven temperature to 375&deg; and continue roasting until the total roasting time
   amounts to 15 - 20 minutes per approximate pound of chicken. For example, a 5 pound chicken should be roasted for a
   total of 120 minutes (20 * 5), so 90 minutes (120 - 30) at this stage.
9. Remove the chicken from the oven, cover with foil, and let rest for 15 minutes before carving to serve

## Variations

* Different dry and wet rubs can be applied and/or aromatic fresh herbs can be added to the stuffing
* A bed of vegetables can be placed beneath the bird in the roasting pan. If so, they should, at least, be tossed in a
  light amount of oil and seasoning.
