# Mushroom, Beef &amp; Onion Hand Pies

## Ingredients

* 1 tbs cooking fat (butter, lard, oil)
* 1 large yellow onion, chopped
* 1 pint of mushrooms, minced
* 6 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp ground black pepper
* 3 1/2 tsp paprika
* 1/3 tsp fennel seed
* 1 tsp savory or sage, bruised and chopped
* 1 tsp rosemary, minced
* 1 lb ground meat (beef, pork, lamb, venison, bison, etc), crumbled into dice-sized chunks
* 1/3 batch of [short crust pastry dough](/breads/short_crust_pastry_dough.md)

## Directions

### Filling

1. Heat cooking fat in a large skillet over medium-low heat
2. Add the onions and cook to sweat out as much moisture as possible
3. Turn heat up to medium-high, then add mushrooms and garlic, stirring for another 3-4 minutes
4. Add herbs and seasonings and stir another 2 minutes until fragrant
5. Set mixture aside, and add the meat to brown
6. Once browned, reduce the heat and use the spatula to break the chunks into small crumbles
7. Returns vegetable mix to pan and combine ingredients well
8. Take off heat and allow to cool

### Assembly

1. Preheat oven to 375&deg;
2. Lightly flour working surface
3. Roll out the dough to 1/4" - 1/8". Thinner if they're going to be eaten right away; thicker for travel
4. Use a cutter to make individual rounds, 2 for each pie
5. Densely pack 2 tbs of filling onto a bottom round
6. Tightly stretch a second round over top, and crimp with a fork
7. Dock a couple holes on top
8. Arrange onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet with a couple inches between each
9. Optionally apply an egg wash at this time
10. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown
