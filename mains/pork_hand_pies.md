# Pork Hand Pies

## Ingredients

* 1 lb. pork country sausage, crumbled
* 1 onion, chopped
* 2 tbs flour
* 1/2 tbs thyme
* 1/2 tsp allspice
* 1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
* pinch ground nutmeg
* pinch coarse (like Kosher) salt
* 1/3 batch [short crust pastry](/breads/short_crust_pastry.md)

## Directions

### Filling

1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and brown the sausage
2. Using a slotted spoon, set aside the sausage
3. Reduce heat to medium-low and add onions
4. Sweat the onions until most of the steam has been been released
5. Return meat to skillet and add the seasonings, mixing well
6. Cook another couple minutes until fragrant
7. A little at a time, sprinkle in and mix the flour to thicken the gravy
8. Take off of heat and allow to cool

### Assembly

1. Preheat oven to 375&deg;
2. Lightly flour working surface
3. Roll out the dough to 1/4" - 1/8". Thinner if they're going to be eaten right away; thicker for travel
4. Use a cutter to make individual rounds, 2 for each pie
5. Densely pack 2 tbs of filling onto a bottom round
6. Tightly stretch a second round over top, and crimp with a fork
7. Dock a couple holes on top
8. Arrange onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet with a couple inches between each
9. Optionally apply an egg wash at this time
10. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown
