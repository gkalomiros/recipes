# Cornbread

## Ingredients

### Batter

#### Dry

* 1 cup yellow cornmeal
* 1/2 cup AP flour
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tbs baking powder

#### Wet

* 1 cup buttermilk
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 egg

#### Wet (continued)

* 1/2 tsp baking soda

#### Finishing

* 1/4 cup butter

### Cooking Fat

* 2 tbs butter/lard/bacon grease

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 450&deg;
2. In a medium bowl, whisk to combine the cornmeal, flour, salt, and baking powder.
3. In a smaller bowl, with a fork, combine the buttermilk, milk, and egg.
4. Mix the baking soda into the wet ingredients.
5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry, and stir to combine.
6. Melt the quarter cup of butter and slowly mix it into the batter until just combined.
7. Heat iron skillet over medium heat with remaining fat.
8. Once hot, pour the batter into the skillet, and spread to even it out.
9. Allow skillet to sizzle for 1 to 2 minutes.
10. Place the skillet into the oven and bake for 20 to 25 minutes until golden with crispy edges.
