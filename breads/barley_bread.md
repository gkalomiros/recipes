# Barley Bread

## Ingredients

### Dry

* 500g whole grain wheat flour
* 250g barley flour
* 1/2 tbs salt

### Wet

* 15g|1/2oz active dry yeast
* 1/3 cup brown ale
* 2 cups lukewarm water (100 - 110 &deg;F)
* 2 tsp honey

## Directions

1. In a large warmed mixing bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients
2. In a smaller bowl, stir the honey into 1 1/2 cups of the water until dissolved
3. Stir in the yeast until the grains become indistinct
4. Gently stir in the ale to prevent bubbling over
5. Cover the bowl and allow the yeast to awaken for 2 - 10 minutes. Mixture should be bubbly
6. Stir wet ingredients into dry until a firm dough comes together, adding the remaining water as needed
7. Kneed the dough a little to form a little elasticity, form a ball
8. Lightly oil the mixing bowl before returning the dough ball
9. Cover the bowl with a towel and leave in a warm, dry place to prove until it doubles in size, about an hour or two
10. Punch down the dough, form two loaves, placing into bread tins, and cross-cut the top
11. Preheat oven to 450&deg;
12. Cover the loaves again, and allow to rest while the oven comes up to temperature
13. Bake for 20-25 minutes, turn out onto a wire rack, cover with a towel, and allow to cool completely before cutting
