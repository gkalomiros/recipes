# Lemon Potatoes (Patates Lemonates)

## Ingredients

* 8 medium Yukon Gold potatoes, washed and cut into wedges
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 1/4 cup water
* 1 or 2 lemons, juiced
* 1 tbs dried oregano
* 2+ garlic cloves, crushed and minced
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1 tbs dijon mustard

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400&deg;
2. Put potatoes in large bowl
3. Whisk remaining ingredients in a small bowl
4. Pour mixture over potatoes and toss to coat
5. Pour potatoes in a single layer into a large baking dish
6. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes
7. Remove foil, reduce heat to 375&deg;, and bake another 30 minutes
