# Mojo Sauce/Marinade

## Ingredients

* 1 orange, juiced
* 2 limes, juiced
* 8 garlic cloves, coarsely crushed for maridate, pureed for sauce
* 1/3 cup olive oil
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 1/2 tsp cumin
* 1/4 tsp black pepper
* salt

## Directions

1. Mix measured ingredients in a bowl, add salt to taste, more if for marinade
2. If using as a sauce, allow to rest for at least an hour
