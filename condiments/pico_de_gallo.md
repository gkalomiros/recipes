# Pico de Gallo

## Ingredients

* 2-3 slicing tomatoes, seeded and chopped
* 1 white onion, chopped
* 1 jalepe&ntilde;o pepper, chopped fine
* 1 serrano pepper, chopped fine
* 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
* salt

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients in a bowl

## Notes

* More or different peppers can be added for more heat
* Removing the seeds and white membrane from inside the peppers can keep the flavor while reducing the heat by a lot
* Yellow onion can be used in a pinch, but won't have the same flavor
