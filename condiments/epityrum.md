# Epityrum

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 tbs cilantro, finely chopped
* 1 1/2 tbs fennel leaf, finely chopped
* 1 1/2 tbs fresh mint, finely chopped
* 2 tsp tarragon, finely chopped
* 1 tsp cumin
* 2 tbs red wine vinegar
* 1/4 cup olive oil
* 2 cups pitted, brined olives

## Directions

1. Combine herbs, spices, vinegar, and oil in a small bowl
2. Lightly rinse the olive, then pat dry
3. Finely chop the olives for a relish, or mash if making a spread
4. Add olives to bowl and mix well
5. Cover and allow to marinate overnight
6. Serve with mild goat cheese, flat bread, and olive oil
