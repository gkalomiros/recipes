# Cilantro and Lime Salad Dressing

## Ingredients

* 1/4 cup fresh lime juice, and zest from limes
* 1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
* 1/2 tsp ground coriander seed
* 1 tbs dijion mustard
* 1 garlic clove, crushed
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 cup olive oil

## Directions

1. Combine juice, zest, cilantro, coriander, mustard, garlic, and salt in a food processor
2. Pulse to combine
3. Turn processor on and slowly pour oil into the mixture until smooth
