# Red Pepper Relish

## Ingredients

* 2 roasted bell peppers (from jar), sliced into thin strips
* 2 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
* 3 tbs extra virgin olive oil
* 2 tbs fresh Italian parsley, finely chopped
* 2 tbs fresh cilantro, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients into a small bowl, and let marinate at least 15 minutes
