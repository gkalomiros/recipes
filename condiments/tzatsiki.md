# Tzatsiki

## Ingredients

* 4 Persian cucumbers
* 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
* 2 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
* 1 lemon, zested and juiced
* 2 tbs olive oil
* 1 tbs fresh dill, chopped
* 1 tbs fresh mint, chopped
* 1 tsp salt

## Directions

1. As fine as possible, grate the cucumbers
2. Using a muslim cloth, squeeze as much liquid as is possible from the cucumbers[*](#note)
3. In a small bowl, add the garlic, lemon zest and juice, oil, herbs, and salt and mix until well combined
4. Stir the cucumber pulp and seasonings into the yogurt until well mixed

## Note

The juice squeezed from the cucumbers should be kept for other purposes, like cucumber water
