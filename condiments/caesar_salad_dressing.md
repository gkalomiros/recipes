# Caesar Salad Dressing

## Ingredients

* 1 cup olive oil
* 4 egg yolks
* 1 tbs dijon mustard
* 1 tsp worchestershire sauce
* 4 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
* 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

## Directions

1. Add oil, yolks, mustard, worchestershire, and garlic to a small bowl
2. Whisk the mixture until emulsified
3. Slowly add lemon juice while whisking to preserve emulsion
