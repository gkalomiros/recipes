# Guacamole

## Ingredients

* 3-4 large avocados
* 1/2 bunch green onion, finely chopped
* 1/2 white onion, finely diced
* 1/2 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
* 2-3 limes, zested and juiced
* salt

## Directions

1. Halve the avocados, removing the pits
2. Shred out the avocado meat with a fork into a bowl
3. Combine the ingredients in the bowl, being careful not to break down the avocado too much

## Notes

* Peppers can be added for heat, but I prefer to serve mild guacamole with a spicy salsa
