# (Imitation) Cumberland Sauce

## Ingredients

* 1/4 cup red current jelly
* 1/4 cup Worchestershire sauce
* 2 tbs ketchup
* 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
* 1/4 cup butter

## Directions

1. mix jelly, Worchestershire sauce, ketchup, and lemon juice in a medium saucepan
2. bring to boil, then reduce back to low, simmering for 2-3 minutes
3. add butter and stir until fully absorbed
