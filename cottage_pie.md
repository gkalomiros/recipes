# Cottage Pie

## Ingredients

### Filling

* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 onion, chopped
* 1 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed and drained
* 2 tbs fresh parsley, chopped
* 1 tbs Italian seasoning
* 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 2 tbs AP flour
* 1 1/2 cups beef stock
* 1 tbs tomato paste

### Topping

* 4 potatoes, diced
* 1/2 butter, softened
* 1 cup milk

### Finishing

* 1/4 lb cheddar, shredded

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400&deg;
2. Whisk together beef stock and tomato paste in a small bowl, set aside
3. Heat a large sautee pan over medium heat with a little oil
4. Heat a small pot of salted water over high heat, then reduce to a low boil
5. Add potatoes to water and let boil about 15 minutes
6. Crumble beef into small chunks
7. Add the beef, onions, carrots, peas, and a heafty pinch of salt to skillet to brown
8. Add herbs and cinnamon to saute for another minute until fragrent
9. Sprinkle a little flour at a time into mixture to coat while avoiding clumps
10. Slowly add stock mixture to pan, scraping up the bottom until well combined
11. Simmer about 15 minutes until liquid is mostly reduced
12. While meat sauce is simmering, strain the potatoes and return to pot
13. Add butter and milk to potatoes while mashing, breaking up any lumps
14. Whip the potatoes as best as possible while adding salt and pepper to taste
15. Transfer meat sauce to a baking dish
16. Top the meat sauce with the potatoes taking care to spread evenly
17. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top and bake about 25 minutes
